About Protirokkha Foundation

We decided to form Protirokkha Foundation a few years ago with some friends studying in the university. Our main objective was to help people affected by natural calamities every year. After forming the foundation, we got everyone's support and our activities progressed better.We provide shelter, food, daily necessities and financial assistance to the needy people every time. Note that our foundation is a non-profit organization.We hope to continue our activities in the coming days.
